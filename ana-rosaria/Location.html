Location

The guest house is located centrally within a radius of 7 kms from Panjim, 8km from Mapusa, 5 kms from Candolim and 7 kms from Calangute. The Dabolim airport is 36 kms, Tivim railway station is 15 kms, Panjim bus stand is 7 kms and Mapusa bus stand is 8 kms from the guest house. We are located opposite the Corporation Bank, on the way to Candolim\Calangute.
